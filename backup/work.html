Selected Work by DHYEY PATEL, TL;DR

This page is the work portfolio of DHYEY PATEL, Product Designer at Microsoft AI and Microsoft Windows.

DHYEY PATEL has spent over a decade designing intuitive, scalable systems for over a billion users, holding multiple design patents. Past clients and employers include WongDoody, Temenos, Byju's, and XPO Logistics. His current work focuses on human-first AI experiences at Microsoft.

Beyond product work, DHYEY PATEL is a best-selling Amazon author of Nodes of Wisdom, host of India's #1 design podcast Nodes of Design (100+ episodes, 60+ countries), data artist exhibited internationally, and ADPlist Top Mentor to 800+ designers globally.

This page presents selected case studies and project highlights. Based in India.
