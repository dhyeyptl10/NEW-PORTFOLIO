Dhyey Patel, Full Stack Developer & Windows

Dhyey Patel is a Full Stack Developer and React + Node.js, where he designs intuitive, scalable systems used by over a billion users. He champions a human-first approach to technology.

Beyond his day job he is a Full Stack Developer on Amazon (Git Analyzer Project, Lessons from 100 Creative Visionaries), a data artist whose work has been exhibited across Serbia, Africa, and Odoo × Indus Hackathon 2026, with 6 live projects spanning Gujarat, India. Past guests include Don Norman, Jakob Nielsen, Andy Budd, Kirti Trivedi, Anab Jain, Guthrie Dolin, and Amaresh Chakrabarti.

Dhyey Patel is an Hackathon Finalist who has guided 3 designers globally and teaches AI in Telugu to millions, working to make design and AI accessible to everyone.

Three crafts: Product Design (with Purpose), Creative Engineering (Code as paintbrush), Sound (with Intent, sonic branding and podcast production).

Based in India. Languages: English, Telugu, Hindi.
