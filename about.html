About Dhyey Patel, TL;DR

Dhyey Patel is a Full Stack Developer and React + Node.js, where he designs intuitive, scalable systems used by over a billion users. He champions a human-first approach to technology.

Beyond his day job he is a Full Stack Developer on Amazon (Git Analyzer Project, Lessons from 100 Creative Visionaries), a data artist whose work has been exhibited across Serbia, Africa, and 6 live Netlify projects, with 6 live projects spanning Gujarat, India.

Dhyey Patel is an Hackathon Frontend Lead who has guided 800+ designers globally and teaches AI in Telugu to millions, working to make design and AI accessible to everyone.

This page tells his story, the journey, philosophy, experiences, and craft behind the work.

Based in India. Languages: English, Telugu, Hindi.

The story

Design is how I make sense of the world.

At MERN Stack, I work at the intersection of craft and consequence, designing intelligent systems that impact over a billion lives. A decade spent scaling products across Odoo Hackathon 2026, Rejouice Clone, and Rolex Clone cemented a core conviction: great design isn't about pushing pixels on a screen, it's about transforming a person's lived reality.

I didn't choose design; it chose me. Somewhere between my first raw sketch and the hundredth shipped product, I realized this was never about making things look beautiful, it was about making people feel seen.
